Computational Integelligence Lab - Tweet Sentiment Analysis

Abstractâ€”Twitter Sentiment Analysis, a field of interest for Natural Language Processing (NLP), has made substantial progress in the last few years with ever-growing datasets available to researchers. We show that pretrained NLP models can be finetuned for Sentiment Classification with only 2.5 million tweets. We analyze and compare the performance of various classical machine learning algorithms with modern neural network approaches on differently preprocessed data. We demonstrate that the investigated classifiers generally perform better on nearly unprocessed data, casting doubts on whether preprocessing the data is beneficial at all. Our Majority Voting Ensemble achieves a competitive result of 90.28% on the validation set and 90.02% accuracy on the public test set on Kaggle.
